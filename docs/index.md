# Longevity Research Library

This is a curated research interface focused on **longevity enhancement for a 74-year-old male**.

!!! warning "Not medical advice"
    This repository summarizes research for education and clinician discussion. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace medical care.

## Purpose

The goal is to maintain a practical, evidence-focused view of:

- Interventions that may improve healthspan, function, and resilience in older men
- Biomarkers worth tracking with a clinician
- Areas where claims are promising but not yet proven
- Safety issues, contraindications, and medication interactions
- New high-quality research as it emerges

## Default evidence hierarchy

1. Human randomized controlled trials and meta-analyses
2. Large prospective cohort studies
3. Mechanistic human studies
4. Translational animal studies with clear human relevance
5. Expert reviews from reputable clinicians/researchers
6. Hypothesis-generating or speculative material, clearly labeled

## Starting focus areas

- Exercise: resistance, aerobic, VO₂ max, balance, fall prevention
- Protein, nutrition quality, frailty prevention, body composition
- Sleep, circadian health, cognition, mood, social connection
- Cardiometabolic risk: blood pressure, ApoB/LDL-C, insulin resistance
- Bone, muscle, mobility, sarcopenia
- Vaccination, infection resilience, cancer screening
- Geroprotective compounds: rapamycin, metformin, GLP-1s, SGLT2 inhibitors, NAD precursors, senolytics — tracked cautiously
- Diagnostics: DEXA, labs, CGM where appropriate, cardiovascular risk imaging where clinically justified
