# Questions for Clinicians

Use this as a discussion checklist, not as self-treatment guidance.

## Primary care / geriatric medicine

- Are cardiovascular risk factors optimally managed for my age, goals, and side-effect tolerance?
- Are my vaccinations current?
- Are cancer screenings appropriate given my age, health status, and preferences?
- Should I have a structured medication review for deprescribing opportunities?
- Should we screen for sleep apnea, osteoporosis, frailty, fall risk, hearing, vision, or cognition?

## Cardiology / metabolic health

- Should ApoB, Lp(a), CAC scoring, or other risk stratification be considered?
- Are blood pressure targets appropriate, avoiding dizziness/falls?
- Are glucose and insulin-resistance markers concerning?

## Exercise / physical therapy

- What resistance and balance program is safe and progressive for me?
- Are there gait, strength, or mobility limitations that need supervised PT?

## Geroscience / longevity clinic questions

- Which recommendations are supported by human outcome data?
- What are the risks, monitoring plan, and stopping criteria?
- Could this intervention interact with current medications or conditions?
- Are there clinical trials that are more appropriate than off-label use?
