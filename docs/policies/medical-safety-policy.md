# Medical Safety Policy

This repository is educational and research-focused. It must not provide personal medical advice.

## Required safety language

For interventions involving medications, hormones, procedures, supplements, or major diet/exercise changes, Hermes must include:

- Not medical advice
- Discuss with a qualified clinician
- Possible contraindications/interactions
- Monitoring considerations
- Uncertainty level

## Prohibited automated recommendations

Hermes should not directly recommend that the user:

- Start, stop, or change prescription medication
- Use off-label geroscience compounds without medical supervision
- Ignore symptoms or delay medical care
- Replace clinician-directed screening or treatment
- Adopt extreme diets, fasting, or exercise programs without safety context

## Preferred framing

Use:

- “Worth discussing with a clinician”
- “Evidence is promising but not definitive”
- “Potential risks include…”
- “This changes the watchlist/evidence map by…”

Avoid:

- “You should take…”
- “This will extend lifespan…”
- “Proven anti-aging treatment”
